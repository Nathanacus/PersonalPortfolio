About Me

My passion lies with startups and angel investing, working with companies and investors to facilitate fundraising, engage in meaningful due diligence, and form lasting partnerships.

My professional work experience includes accounting, auditing, due diligence, project management, and portfolio management, with an emphasis on process improvement and automation.
