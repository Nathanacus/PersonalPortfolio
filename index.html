About Me

My passion lies with startups and angel investing, working with companies and investors to facilitate fundraising, engage in meaningful due diligence, and form lasting partnerships.

I received my JD, MBA, and BA from Willamette University, where I was actively involved with the Willamette Angel Fund and the Business Law Clinic for two years. I was admitted to the Oregon State Bar in October 2019 and was licensed as a Washington CPA in July 2021. I have completed Levels I and II of the CFA Program. My professional work experience includes accounting, auditing, due diligence, project management, and portfolio management, with an emphasis on process improvement and automation.
